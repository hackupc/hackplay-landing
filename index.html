ABOUT

WHAT IS HackPLAY?

HackPLAY is a group of technical talks focused on videogames and networking.

This event will be a great opportunity to learn about videogames and connect with other restless minds in this amazing world. The result is an environment that facilitates relationships between the innovative companies and talent they need to make their vision a reality.
